About & FAQ

What is the goal of the app?

Give families a calm place to record life stories in their own voice. Everything is designed to be soft, simple, and respectful.


How do I start?
  1. Click Record and sign in if prompted.
  2. Pick a language, tap a prompt (optional), and press the big round button.
  3. Stop when you’re done — the text appears, and you can save it as a story.

How does transcription work?

We use chunked uploads to OpenAI Whisper for robust Dutch/French/Spanish/English transcription. If you go offline, we buffer and retry safely.
