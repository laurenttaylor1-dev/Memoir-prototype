About & FAQ

What is this? Memoir App helps families record and preserve life stories. It uses accurate speech-to-text (Whisper) and keeps your content safe in a private library.

How to start? Click Record, pick a language, choose a topic (or free mode), and press the big round button. Save when you’re done.

Where are my stories? Click My Stories to view, rewrite with AI, download, or manage your library.
