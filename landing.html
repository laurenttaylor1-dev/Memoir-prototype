FAQ

How do I record a story?

Go to Record, tap the round button, and start speaking. Your words are transcribed automatically.

What is AI rewrite?

Premium users can enhance their stories with smoother language, while keeping their authentic voice.

How do I share with family?

How do subscriptions work?
